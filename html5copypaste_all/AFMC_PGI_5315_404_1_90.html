AFMC PGI 5315.404-1-90 Pricing Assistance or Pricing Assistance Waiver

(a)For LCMC (Wright-Patterson AFB), source selection pricing assistance may be provided for structuring instructions to offerors and evaluation criteria, as needed depending on resource constraints. Full-time pricing assistance during source selection is not required.
