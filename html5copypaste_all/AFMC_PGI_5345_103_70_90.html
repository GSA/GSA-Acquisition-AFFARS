AFMC PGI 5345.103-70-90 Furnishing Government Property to Contractors

Before awarding any cost reimbursement, time-and-material, or any other contract action that includes GFP or FAR 52.245-1, the Contracting Officer should document compliance with the elements identified in the GFP Pre-Award Checklist .  While use of the checklist is highly recommended, contracting activities may leverage existing procedures and documentation to satisfy this requirement in an efficient manner.  HQ AFMC/PK may request a copy of the Pre-Award Checklist upon identification of a non-compliant contract action.
