AFMC PGI 5333.103 Protests to the Agency

(f)(1) A decision to award a contract in the face of a protest may be approved by the COCO after review by the local legal office.

(f)(3) A decision to continue performance in the face of a protest may be approved by the COCO after review by the local legal office.
