AFMC PGI 5317.204-90 Contracts

Option clauses designed for contracts that include technical achievement milestones, such as first article approval, qualification test approval or other test and/or development demonstration events should use event-driven milestones for the option exercise window (e.g., completion of event XYZ or XX days after completion of event XYZ) in lieu of calendar-based dates (e.g., 15 Mar 2007).
