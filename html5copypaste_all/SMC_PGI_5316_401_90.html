SMC PGI 5316.401-90 General

(a) Contracting officers are strongly encouraged to document the determination to use an incentive- or award-fee type contract (or CLIN) using the SMC Award Fee and Incentive Fee D&F templates.

(b) Approval authorities for incentive fee plans and award fee plans are found in AFPEO/SP memo, SMC Award Fee and Incentive Plan Approval Authorities , dated 15 May 19.
