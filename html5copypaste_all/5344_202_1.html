5344.202-1 Responsibilities

(a) For SMC, the contracting officer shall withhold subcontract consent responsibility from the administering contracting officer for all space program contracts (See AFFARS 5302 ), and shall review and disposition consent requests, including those submitted pursuant to SMC Class Deviation of FAR clause 52.244-2 , Subcontracts, paragraph (k).
