5309.105-1 Obtaining Information

(a)(i) For SMC, prior to performing any of the actions listed in 5309.103(b)(iii), the contracting officer shall determine whether or not the solicitation or contract is a space program solicitation or contract (See AFFARS 5302 ) and verify whether or not the contractor(s), in whole or in part, is listed on the CRWL . The CRWL includes documentation in support of decision(s) to place the contractor(s) on the CRWL.

See SMC PGI 5309.105-1 .
