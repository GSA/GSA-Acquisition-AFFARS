AFMC PGI 5315.207 Handling Proposals and Information

(a) If a package contains more than one proposal, the buyer or contracting officer should stamp each envelope with the time and date of delivery. If a proposal is delivered to the wrong office, the recipient should stamp the time and date received, and then deliver the proposal to the intended office immediately.
