USAFA PGI 5301.90 Clearance

The USAFA SCO delegates clearance authority to the COCO for actions valued at less than $10M. Contracting Officers should refer to the USAFA/PK authorities and delegations site concerning specific COCO designations, delegations and authorities at https://cs2.eis.af.mil/sites/13093/PP/USAFA_PK/_layouts/15/start.aspx#/SitePages/Home.aspx .
