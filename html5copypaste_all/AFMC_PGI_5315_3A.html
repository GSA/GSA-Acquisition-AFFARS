AFMC PGI 5315.3 (1.4.2.2.3) Verification of Correspondence Going to Offeror

All source selection teams should consider use of the “ Verification of Correspondence Going to Offeror ” checklist for correspondence going to offerors, whether in hard copy or electronic format, and for information being posted to a website accessible by offerors.
