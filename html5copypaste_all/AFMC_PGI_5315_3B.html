AFMC PGI 5315.3 (1.4.6.1) Government Advisors

Some Operating Locations/Centers assign a Source Selection Support Advisor from the local Acquisition Center of Excellence (ACE) and/or local Clearance and Program Support Office to serve in an advisory capacity to the SSA and the Source Selection Team (SST). When a Source Selection Support Advisor is not assigned or not available, teams should refer to the AFFARS Library Part 5315 , for examples, templates, forms and useful guidance.
