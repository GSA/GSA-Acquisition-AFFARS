AFMC PGI 5305.303 Announcement of Contract Awards

1279 SCO Reporting . For AFRL, if an action is submitted for HQ AFRL/PK Contract Clearance, the Contracting Officer is deemed to have met the requirement for providing a courtesy copy of the 1279 report to the SCO. If Contract Clearance is not applicable to an action valued at $50M or above, or the requirement for contract clearance for said action has been waived; the local contracting office will include notification of the anticipated award in their Monthly Activity Report preceding the award, but no fewer than 3 days prior to the expected date of award.
