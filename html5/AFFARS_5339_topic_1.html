5339.7201-90 Data Servers/Centers Approval Process

Purchase Request packages for data servers/centers must include SAF/CIO A6 approval to be accepted as valid for procurement action by a contracting officer. See AFGM 2018-17-02 , Air Force Enterprise Information Technology Management, Section 4, paragraph 4.3.
