AFMC PGI 5339 Acquisition of Information Technology

AFMC PGI 5339.101-90 Policy

(a) Make all purchases of desktop and laptop computers through AFWay or the ITCC-developed Quarterly Enterprise Buy, per AFMAN 17-1203 , Information Technology (IT) Asset Management (ITAM).

(b) NETCENTS/NETCENTS 2 is used for all requirements for products and services associated with the design, engineering, integration, installation and configuration of Air Force networks and network infrastructure per AFMAN 17-1203 . See AF NETCENTS site for additional details.

(c) Buy all desktop and laptop computers paid for with the government purchase card through AFWay per AFI 64-117 , Government Purchase Card Program.
