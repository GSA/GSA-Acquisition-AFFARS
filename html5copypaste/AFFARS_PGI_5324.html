SMC PGI 5324 — Protection of Privacy and Freedom of Information

SMC PGI 5324.203   Policy 

     (a) The 61st Communications Squadron administers the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) program for Los Angeles AFB (including SMC contracting workload accomplished at Kirtland AFB and Peterson AFB) and processes requests for SMC contract records under the provisions of the Freedom of Information Act.  The Los Angeles FOIA page is located at: 

https://insidesmc.losangeles.af.mil/sites/61cs/services/pafoiaprogram/default.aspx
