AFMC PGI 5301.602-2 Responsibilities

(c)(i)(A) In addition to the matters listed in AFFARS 5301.602-2(c), contracting officers should obtain legal review for the following:

(20) Procurement integrity issues

(21) Organizational conflict of interest issues

(22) Matters related to defective pricing

(23) Advance or pre-contract cost agreements

(24) Undefinitized contract actions and change orders (initial issuance and definitization).

(c)(i)(C) See Class Deviation - Legal Review-AFLCMC/PK 19-02
